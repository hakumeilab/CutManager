# CutManager Website Design Prompt

This site should follow a warm, minimal, Notion-inspired landing-page style.

## Source
- Reference: `https://github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md`
- Base inspiration: `design-md/notion/DESIGN.md`

## Visual direction
- Warm neutral palette, not cold gray.
- White and warm-white surfaces with near-black text.
- Very thin borders instead of heavy outlines.
- Depth should be soft and quiet, with low-contrast shadows.
- The page should feel calm, practical, and editorial.

## Color roles
- Background: `#ffffff`
- Alternate background: `#f6f5f4`
- Primary text: `rgba(0,0,0,0.95)`
- Secondary text: `#615d59`
- Border: `1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`
- Accent: `#0075de`
- Accent hover: `#005bab`
- Badge background: `#f2f9ff`
- Badge text: `#097fe8`

## Typography
- Use `Inter` or a close system fallback.
- Large headlines should be compact and slightly tight.
- Body text should stay short and readable.
- Avoid oversized labels and decorative display treatment.

## Layout
- Max width around `1100px` to `1200px`.
- Large top and bottom whitespace.
- Keep sections few and simple.
- Prefer 1-column hero, then compact 2-3 column support content.
- Avoid dense grids and long paragraphs.

## Components
- Primary button: blue, rectangular with subtle radius.
- Secondary button: soft neutral background.
- Cards: white, thin border, soft shadow, radius `12px`.
- Badges: full pill, small, blue-tinted.

## Content guidance
- Keep copy short.
- One strong hero statement.
- One short supporting paragraph.
- A few concise feature bullets only.
- Keep download/action area obvious.

## Avoid
- Loud gradients.
- Heavy glassmorphism.
- Large decorative blobs.
- Over-explaining features.
- Too many cards or sections.
